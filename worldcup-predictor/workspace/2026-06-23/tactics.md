# tactics — 2026-06-23

## Algeria vs Jordan (11:00, Levi's Stadium Santa Clara)
- **Verdict:** Algeria edge it, a grind, ~2-1.
- **Confidence:** medium
- **Basis:** Petković's Algeria in 4-2-3-1→4-3-3 with flying fullbacks (Aït-Nouri, Belghali) and half-space creators (Mahrez inverting, Amoura/Maza). Jordan (Ammouta) defend in a compact 3-4-3→5-4-1 block — the shell that gave 4 clean sheets in 6 at the Asian Cup. Algeria must break a low block (the Argentina problem) but Jordan lack Argentina's press/bodies; Algeria's individual quality tells over 90'. The crack: Algeria's slow CB pairing + 3 green keepers (1 clean sheet in last 13 WC games) vs Al-Taamari's pace on the counter — the decisive duel is Al-Taamari vs the highest Algerian fullback. If Petković manages rest-defence, Algeria win late; if gung-ho, Jordan steal a point. Jordan's old flaw (ball progression under pressure) tilts it further to Algeria.
