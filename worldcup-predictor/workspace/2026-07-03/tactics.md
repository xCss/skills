- **Verdict:** Portugal, but collar buttoned tight — the banana skin of the round. Croatia can't win a track meet but Modrić/Kovačić can strangle the game and drag Portugal into the swamp they hate.
- **Confidence:** medium
- **Predicted margin:** 2-1 Portugal (after labouring; extra time live)
- **Basis:** Match hinges on tempo + the opening goal. Croatia's plan: Modrić drops deep, slows to a crawl, lets Portugal's impatience rot them — exactly what Colombia did in the 0-0 (Portugal pressed poorly, "no intention to play forward"). - **Key duel:** Vitinha vs Modrić — the tempo battle. Whoever wins the first 20 min of rhythm likely wins.
