# Tactics Report — 2026-07-07

## Match 1: Portugal vs Spain

### Formation matchup
**Spain 4-3-3 possession press vs Portugal 4-2-3-1 compact counter**
- Spain (de la Fuente): high possession, aggressive press, Pedri/Rodri control, Yamal width+pace, full-backs high.
- Portugal (Martínez): double pivot shields defense, Bruno Fernandes #10, Ronaldo focal point, sits deeper, counters.

### Key duels
1. **Yamal's pace vs João Cancelo (POR LB)** — Yamal torched older fullbacks in group stage.
2. **Rodri (ESP DM) vs Bruno Fernandes** — if Rodri shadows Bruno, Portugal's counters die.
3. **Laporte/Le Normand vs Ronaldo** — deny service, force him wide.
4. **Pedri's press resistance vs Portugal's double pivot.**

### Set piece analysis
- **Spain: HIGH** (Pedro Porro FK goal R32, Morata aerial, rehearsed routines)
- **Portugal: MEDIUM** (Ronaldo FKs declining, Bruno corners, drew penalty vs Croatia)

### Pick: **Spain 1-0 (or 1-1 → Spain in ET/pens)**
Portugal's attack toothless (Ronaldo 1 pen, Bruno/Vitinha subbed 60' vs Croatia). Spain's zero-concession defense strangles Portugal's weak creativity. Low-scoring grind decided by set piece or Yamal/Oyarzabal moment.

## Match 2: USA vs Belgium

### Formation matchup (USA without Balogun)
**USA 4-3-3 high press (weakened attack) vs Belgium 3-4-2-1 wing-back**
- USA (Pochettino): high press, Pulisic cuts inside, athletic midfield. **Critical: Balogun (top scorer) suspended → Pepi/Wright downgraded finishers.**
- Belgium (Martínez): wing-backs width, De Bruyne+Trossard #10s, Lukaku lone striker, back three sits deep and counters.

### Key duels
1. **Pulisic vs Castagne (BEL RWB)** — Pulisic USA's most dangerous with Balogun out.
2. **Tyler Adams (USA DM) vs De Bruyne** — Adams must stop KDB through-balls to Lukaku.
3. **Pepi/Wright vs Vertonghen/Alderweireld** — Belgium CBs slow (35+) but experienced; neither replacement replicates Balogun.
4. **USA high line vs Lukaku's runs** — if line caught flat, Lukaku punishes with pace/power.

### Set piece analysis
- **Belgium: VERY HIGH** (Lukaku elite aerial, De Bruyne pinpoint delivery)
- **USA: MEDIUM** (Pulisic FKs, McKennie/Zimmerman aerial)

### Pick: **Belgium 2-1**
USA loses its head (Balogun). Pepi/Wright can't replicate finishing. USA press + crowd create chances but without clinical finishing can't convert. Belgium's maturity + Lukaku "big game" mentality (87' equalizer vs Senegal) + De Bruyne unlocking high line = quality gap in final third. Belgium sits, absorbs, counters with precision.
